Our Story

Hot Off The Grill, Straight From Tanay

Family-run, fire-cooked, and proudly local — every plate of sisig and bagnet is cooked fresh to order.

Clark's Crispy Sisig Bagnet started as a small weekend stall serving the hungry hikers and locals around Tanay, Rizal. What began with one sizzling pan has grown into a daily favorite — crispy on the outside, juicy and packed with flavor on the inside.

Every order is cooked fresh on the spot using time-tested recipes passed down in the family, with no shortcuts and no microwaves — just real fire, real pork, and real flavor.
